JAMB Practice App

Our JAMB Practice App is designed to help students prepare effectively for the UTME examination. With thousands of past questions, detailed solutions, and simulated exam environment, you can be well-prepared for your JAMB examination.

Key Features

  • Over 10,000 past questions from 1978 to date
  • Detailed solutions and explanations for all questions
  • Real exam simulation with timer
  • Performance tracking and analysis
  • Offline access to questions and solutions
  • Regular updates with new questions

Installation Guide

Download the App

Click on any of the download buttons above to get the app. If you're downloading the APK file, make sure to enable "Unknown Sources" in your device settings.

Install the App

Open the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions to install the app on your device.

Register and Login

Open the app and create a new account with your email and phone number. You can also log in if you already have an account.

Start Practicing

Select your subjects and start practicing with past questions. You can track your progress and see your performance statistics.
