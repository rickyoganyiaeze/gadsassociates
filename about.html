Who We Are

GADS Associates is a leading educational center established with the mission to provide quality educational services to students in Nigeria. The Academy was established in 2004 as an evening tutorial centre but in 2007 we upgraded to a full fledge tutorial centre that runs both morning and evening session.

Our center is equipped with modern facilities and staffed by highly qualified professionals who are passionate about education and committed to helping students achieve their academic and career goals.

Over the years, we have successfully registered thousands of JAMB candidates and coached numerous ICAN students to pass their examinations at various levels. Our computer training programs have empowered many with essential digital skills needed in today's technology-driven world.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional educational services that empower students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to excel in their academic pursuits and professional careers. We are committed to delivering high-quality coaching, registration support, and computer training through innovative teaching methods and a student-centered approach.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred educational center in Nigeria, recognized for excellence in JAMB registration, ICAN coaching, and computer training. We aim to continuously expand our reach and impact, helping thousands of students annually to achieve their educational goals and contribute positively to national development.
